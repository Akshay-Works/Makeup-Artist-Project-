About the Salon

Crafting Bridal Beauty
With Soul & Precision

Welcome to Ranjna Beauty Salon & Academy — your trusted destination for bridal makeup, Mehendi, haircare, facials, waxing, and beauty training in the heart of Range Hills, Pune.

With years of dedicated service, our salon has earned the love and trust of hundreds of clients across Pune. We believe beauty is not just about appearance — it's about how you feel. Every service we offer is delivered with care, skill, and a personal touch that makes you feel truly special.

Our team is trained in the latest beauty techniques — from HD and airbrush makeup to traditional Mehendi artistry. We also run a beauty academy where we train the next generation of professional makeup artists and beauticians in Pune.

How It Works

Your Journey to
Bridal Perfection

A seamless, stress-free process designed around you — from first inquiry to your wedding morning.

01

Inquiry

Reach out via WhatsApp or the contact form with your wedding date, location, and requirements. We respond within 2 hours.

02

Consultation

A personalised 30-minute consultation (in-person or video call) to discuss your vision, skin type, attire, and inspirations.

03

Trial Session

We do a full trial run of your bridal look. This is your chance to refine details — lighting, intensity, lip colour — until everything is perfect.

04

Booking Confirmed

Your date is locked in with an advance payment. You receive a confirmation and a detailed pre-wedding skin care guide personalised for your skin type.

05

Wedding Day Magic

I arrive at your location with a full luxury kit, professional lighting setup, and a calm, joyful energy — so your morning is as beautiful as the evening ahead.

Common Questions

Everything You
Need to Know

I recommend booking at least 4–6 months in advance for wedding season dates (November–February). Peak dates fill up quickly. For off-season weddings, 2–3 months is usually sufficient. The sooner you inquire, the better — to avoid disappointment.

A 30% advance is required to confirm your booking and hold your date exclusively. The remaining balance is due 7 days before your wedding. We accept bank transfer, UPI (GPay, PhonePe), and cash.

Yes! I travel to Mumbai, Nashik, Aurangabad, Kolhapur, and other Maharashtra cities. Outstation bookings include travel, accommodation, and a daily allowance. Please enquire for a custom quote based on your location.

The trial session is a complete, full-face bridal makeup application to test the final look. We'll check how the makeup wears on your skin throughout the day, refine colours and intensity, and photograph the look for reference on your wedding day. Trial sessions are held 2–4 weeks before the wedding.

Cancellations 30+ days before the event receive a full advance refund. Cancellations 15–30 days prior receive a 50% refund. Cancellations within 15 days of the event are non-refundable, as the date has been held exclusively for you. Date changes are accommodated subject to availability.

I use MAC, Charlotte Tilbury, NARS, Bobbi Brown, Huda Beauty, and Kryolan — all dermatologically tested and professional-grade products. If you have known allergies, please inform me during the consultation so I can adjust products. A patch test can always be done during the trial session.

Bridal makeup typically takes 2.5–3.5 hours, including skincare prep, base application, eyes, lips, and setting. Bridesmaids/family members take 45–60 minutes each. I always recommend building in an extra hour of buffer time in your wedding morning schedule.

Yes! All Premium and Luxury packages include bridal hairstyling. I offer traditional updos, floral setups, braids, and contemporary styles that complement your look and attire. Hair accessories and florals can be incorporated on request.
